The Unseen Health Challenges in the Hotel Industry

Management & Executives
Management & Executives

→ High blood pressure, diabetes, and cardiac risk from sedentary lifestyle and stress.

→ Skipped regular checkups due to busy schedules, leading to late diagnoses.

→ Insomnia, mood swings, and reduced productivity from round-the-clock responsibilities.
Supervisors & Admin Staff
Supervisors & Admin Staff

→ Burnout, anxiety, and reduced focus from managing operations and people.

→ Neck stiffness, back pain, and posture-related issues due to desk jobs.

→ Digestive problems, fatigue, and weight fluctuations due to erratic schedules.
Frontline Workers
Frontline Workers

→ Muscle fatigue, back pain, and varicose veins from long hours on foot.

→ Respiratory issues, skin irritation, and dehydration from kitchens and cleaning agents.

→ Higher exposure to infectious diseases due to guest interactions and shared spaces.
